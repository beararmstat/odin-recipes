CHEESECAKE PANCAKES

Cheesecake pancakes topped with strawberries definitely take your breakfast game up a few notches.

Ingredients

  • for the topping:
    • strawberries
    • strawberry jam
      • for the cream cheese swirl
          cream cheese white sugar milk vanilla extract
      • For the pancake batter
          All-purpose flour baking powder baking soda salt an egg buttermilk vegetable oil cooking spray graham cracker crumbs powdered sugar

      Now that you have your ingredients, Here are the steps:

      steps

        Make the strawberry topping. Make the cream cheese swirl. Make the pancake batter. Pour portions of the batter onto the prepared skillet and cook until the edges are golden brown and the batter is bubbling on top. Pipe a swirl of the cream cheese mixture onto each pancake. Coat with cooking spray Flip and cook until golden brown on both sides. Serve with strawberry topping and sprinkle with graham cracker crumbs.

      Now You Have A Delicious Cheesecake Pancake Breakfast!!
